### **The Initiative**
During peak service periods, I led the volunteer operations for the Islamic Society of Pinellas County, tasked with organizing large-scale community services for over 100 daily attendees.

### **Impact & Leadership**
* **Team Management:** Directed a team of youth volunteers, assigning roles and managing shifts to ensure seamless service delivery.
* **Operational Logistics:** Coordinated food distribution and event setup, ensuring resources were allocated efficiently under tight time constraints.
* **Mentorship:** Acted as a primary mentor for younger participants, fostering a culture of service, discipline, and community responsibility.

### **Relevance to Clinical Practice**
This role developed the **operational leadership** and **stress management** skills required to run a busy clinical environment where patient flow and resource management are critical.
